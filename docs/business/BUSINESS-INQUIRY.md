# Voila! Business Inquiry Draft

Status: draft  
Milestone: v0.2.3-public-beta-commercial-positioning

This document contains draft copy for future business inquiries.

---

## Short business positioning

Voila! helps transform PDF manuals and technical documents into structured learning material.

It can be useful for:

- technical training
- internal documentation
- onboarding
- language learning from manuals
- maritime/industrial/engineering learning workflows
- structured study material generation

---

## Possible business offer

A business package may include:

- commercial usage permission
- onboarding session
- custom setup guidance
- custom language pack planning
- custom glossary workflow
- packaging support
- private build support
- priority bug review
- maintenance agreement

---

## Inquiry text

Interested in using Voila! in a business, training, or internal documentation workflow?

Voila! is currently in public beta, and the final commercial model is still being evaluated.

For business use, custom deployment, support, or commercial licensing, please contact the maintainer with:

- company name
- intended use case
- number of users
- document types
- languages needed
- whether offline/local usage is required
- expected support needs
- billing country

Business usage terms are handled separately and are not automatically granted by the public beta.

---

## What not to promise yet

Do not promise yet:

- SLA
- guaranteed support hours
- certified compliance
- enterprise security
- automatic updates
- signed installer
- unlimited commercial rights
- white-label usage
- redistribution rights

These can be added later if a formal Business plan is created.

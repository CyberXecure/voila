## Value proposition

PDFs are useful, but they are not always easy to study.

Voila turns a static document into a guided learning workflow. Upload a PDF, generate a course, review lessons, inspect figures, study recommended questions, and track your progress.

Voila is built for people who learn from technical manuals, textbooks, training documents, academic PDFs, documentation, reports, and long-form PDF material.

## Feature explanations

### PDF to structured course

Voila converts PDF documents into structured learning material, including lessons, study questions, figures, and progress data.

### Local-first beta workflow

Voila runs locally during beta testing. Testers can try it without creating a cloud account.

### OCR support

Voila includes OCR-assisted workflows for documents where text extraction is incomplete or imperfect.

### Lesson generation

Generated lessons make long documents easier to navigate, review, and study.

### Figure extraction

Figures are extracted and displayed separately, making visual material easier to understand.

### Crop editor

Crop tools help review and improve extracted figure regions and OCR-related content.

### Study mode

Study mode provides recommended questions and lets users review concepts interactively.

### Progress tracking

Progress tools help users see what they completed and what needs review.

> Maintenance of traps
> A defective trap wastes steam and fails to ensure adequate heating. Poor heater
> performance can sometimes be traced to a defective steam trap. One remedy is
> to close in the steam outlet valve to perhaps half or quarter of a tum. Most traps
> can be regularly opened for inspection. The only exception is the
> thermodynamic type. When operating correctly this gives a characteristic
> 'dick', usually at intervals of between 20 and 30 s so that its performance can be
> checked simply by listening.

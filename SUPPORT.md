# Support Voila!

Voila! is currently in public beta.

The project is public for evaluation, feedback, and early testing. The final licensing and commercial model are still under review.

## Ways to support

For now, the best ways to support Voila! are:

- test the public beta
- report issues clearly
- suggest practical improvements
- share real use cases
- sponsor development when a funding link becomes available
- purchase a supporter/pro package when published

## Support expectations

Current public beta support is best-effort.

There is currently no guaranteed response time, SLA, or commercial support contract unless agreed separately in writing.

## Commercial interest

For business use, custom language packs, internal deployment, training workflows, or paid support, please contact the maintainer.

## License status

Until a license is explicitly added to this repository, do not assume that Voila! is MIT, Apache, GPL, or otherwise open-source licensed.

The final license and commercial model are under evaluation.
